Section V

Technology — Motion Capture & Real-Time

These are some of the tools that we have researched for capturing the puppeteers. They range from A.I. technology for motion capture, to using sensors, or suits to capture data that can then be processed digitally. Suits and AI technology, as far as I can see have been developed almost exclusively to capture human forms, so it is unclear how they will work with puppets that have different non-human shapes.

It is conceivable that we could build a skeleton of the rabbits with sensors or add sensors to the puppets that could capture motion data that can be manipulated in Blender or Unreal or other software.

Move.ai

Markerless AI mocap from standard cameras. Gen 2 (March 2025). Real-time via disguise "Invisible" bundle. No suits required.
